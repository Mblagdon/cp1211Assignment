Toronto Maple Leafs

The Toronto Maple Leafs are an ice hockey team, and are known as an original six team in the NHL, based out of Toronto, Ontario. They were founded in 1917, when they were known as the Toronto Arenas, and became the Toronto St. Pat's in 1919, and in 1927 became the Maple Leafs. The Toronto Maple Leafs have won 13 stanley cups, second only to rivals Montreal Canadiens. However, the team has not won a stanley cup since the 1966-1967 season. This drought has become the longest in league history. The team is known for having four main rivals, three stemming from the other original six teams (the Boston Bruins, Montreal Canadiens, and Detroit Red Wings) and the fourth being the Ottawa Senators. The teams main colors for jerseys are blue and white.
